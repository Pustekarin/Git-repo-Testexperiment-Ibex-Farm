Welcome!

You have been invited to take part in this experiment because you are a native speaker of either English, German or Polish. If you are a native speaker of Polish or German you should have a good knowledge of English since the following experiment will be carried out in English.

You can expect this experiment to take about 10 minutes.

Instructions

In this experiment you will be required to read a number of English sentences one word at a time.
  • You can advance to the next word by pressing the space bar.

  • Read the sentences at your normal pace.

  • Pay attention to what you are reading, your understanding of the content will be checked with comprehension questions.

  • Please do not pause while you are reading a sentence. The computer will record the reaction time for each time you press the space bar.

  • You may take a break in between sentences or when the question is displayed if you wish to do so.
  • Please do not refresh the browser during the experiment or you will be redirected back to this intro page!

Privacy statement

If you agree to take part in this experiment you will be asked for some background information like very general demographical data and language background before the experiment starts. Note that this experiment is completely anonymous and none of the information given can be tracked back to you. Your participation in this study is completely voluntary and you can withdraw from it at any time by closing the browser.
