Welcome!

You have been invited to take part in this experiment because you are a native speaker of either English, German or Polish. If you are a native speaker of Polish or German you should have a good knowledge of English since the following experiment will be carried out in English.

You can expect this experiment to take about 10-15 minutes.

Instructions

This is a sentence reading experiment using a method called "self-paced reading". In this experiment you will be required to read a number of English sentences one word at a time. You can advance to the next word by pressing the space bar.

Your task is to read the sentences at your usual pace as you would normally read them. Please pay attention to what you are reading, your understanding of the content will be checked with comprehension questions. There are 3 numbered reply options for each question. Please press the according number on your keyboard for the answer you think is correct.
Please do not pause while you are reading a sentence. The computer will record the reaction time for each time you press the space bar. You may take a break in between sentences if you wish so. After each sentence you will be shown the message "Press space bar to continue". Only then will you be shown the next sentence.

Please do not refresh the browser during the experiment or you will be redirected to this intro page!

Privacy statement

If you agree to take part in this experiment you will be asked for some background information like very general demographical data and language background before the experiment starts. Note that this survey is completely anonymous and none of the information given can be tracked back to you. Your participation in this study is completely voluntary and you can withdraw at any time by closing the browser.
